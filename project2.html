Campaign poster created to spread awareness about wildfires.

This project was hosted by the dutch fire brigade. My group came up with a campaign spreading awareness about wildfires in the summer, which are very often caused accidentally by humans.

The concept of the project is a forest scavenger hunt, that leads to a statue memorial of all the animals that lost their lives in the fires. The goal is to encourage people to take a moment and think about how their unawareness can cause so much damage to the innocent living beings.
